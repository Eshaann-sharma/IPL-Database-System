Welcome to IPL Database !!

The Indian Premier League (IPL), also known as the TATA IPL for sponsorship reasons, is a men's Twenty20 (T20) cricket league held annually in India.
Founded by the BCCI in 2007, the league features ten city-based franchise teams. IPL usually takes place during the summer,
between March and May each year.
